Safe Harbor Identity Security

Track, clean up, and secure your online accounts — automatically.

Safe Harbor is a lightweight cybersecurity dashboard for consumers, freelancers, and small teams that helps users secure their online accounts through better identity hygiene, breach monitoring, phishing protection, and multi-factor authentication (MFA) guidance. Safe Harbor acts as a personal security companion that quickly scans for exposed credentials, checks critical accounts for missing MFA, and flags phishing risks – delivering actionable insights and integrating with powerful security tools within minutes of signup.

Key Benefits

Ongoing Breach Monitoring: Real-time alerts when your info appears in new data leaks.
Phishing Scanner: Drop in a suspicious link or message and let AI scan for threats.
Security Score & Report: Personalized insights to help you strengthen your accounts.
MFA Audit: Instantly identify where two-factor authentication is missing and fix it.
Account Discovery: See where your credentials are used and remove what you don’t need.
